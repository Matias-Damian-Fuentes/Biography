About Me

Hello world, here Matías Fuentes

Hello! Nice to meet you! I present myself, I'm Matías, a young argentine Full Stack Web Developer, and THIS is part of one of my projects of my Harvard career! On this occasion they have given us the challenge of making one of our first web pages; an autobiography! But, without feeling entirely satisfied, I've decided to go a little bit further, in order to turn it into part of my own portfolio! Like a kind of CV, but with style~

Well, here are some things about me:

Geek

I have been in front of a computer since I was four, and since then, I just simply could not stop! It was like my place where I could be just myself, create, and do all the things that I love. I've been always interested in technology in general; from something so basic as a simple little bit, to something so big and complex as a fully autonomous AI. Things like that drives me crazy! And precisely by things like that, it's why I just simply love being a programmer <3, and have planned to learn and apply everything as I can from this world.

Big Dreamer

Bill Gates, Larry Page, Linus Torvalds... Those people are great references for me, and helped me to reach where I'm actually are. People like them are the reason why I always wake up in the morning, make me some nice breakfast, and... just type. Trying always to perform, to push myself, to learn something new... and with that, at the end of the day, be able to create. To contribute. And to make something that, hopefully, one day, changes the world to better. Hopefully one day my code reaches and helps to the people who more need it. I have big dreams. And I'm determined to accomplish them.

Hard Work

I like to have control over things. Take the lead and work as a team! And make sure that everything, from the beginning until the end, works. And works well. The half-done work is not enough for me, and even more important, not enough to satisfy what the client needs. However, I don't consider myself a perfectionist, I know my limits. But I really like the well-done work and be part of it, in every aspect of the entire workflow. That's one of the reasons I decided to be a Full Stack Web Developer!

Teamwork

I really love to work on a team, because that's where you learn a lot. When you're alone, and you do everything by yourself aligned by your own perspective, not only you will be overloaded by responsibilities, but also and most importantly I think, it's that you won't have anyone to question your thoughts and how you implement them. We're all humans and make mistakes, and we can learn from them, but the problem is that we don't always realize that we make mistakes. In this world we need each other. Because the teamwork makes the dream work.

Self-Improvement

I always try to perform myself and refine my personality, in order to become a better person in every aspect of my life. When things are not going as well as I would like, often I try to figure it out by myself what I'm doing wrong, and what can I do about it. If I can't handle it by myself, I ask for help on those that I trust, and that I know that they can give me a hand. And if someone is bothered by something that I've done or whatever, I always try to listen to them, understand what it's bothering to them, apologize and behave myself the next time.

Rational & Objective

Something characteristic about me is that I try to face problems with the greatest possible rationality and objectivity possible. I think it's the best approach to solve different kind of problems. Even more in something so methodical and aligned with hard sciences as Computer Sciences could be. And, besides that, it's a kind of mentality approach that I love to apply on. Everything that has to do with logic and exact sciences, I'm just fascinated. For the sake of the work, every time that I face a new challenge, I try to discern my subjectivity from my objectivity, so that it doesn't interfer.
